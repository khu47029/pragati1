# Boutique Portfolio Website

## Direct Answer
A premium local business website should explain services clearly, show proof, provide a fast WhatsApp/contact path, and include FAQ/schema so both customers and AI search systems understand the business.

## Why it matters
Customers check online trust before contacting a local business. A clear website improves confidence and reduces confusion.

## Checklist
- Clear hero headline
- Services section
- Reviews and trust proof
- Gallery or portfolio
- WhatsApp inquiry button
- FAQ section
- Schema markup
- Google Business Profile link

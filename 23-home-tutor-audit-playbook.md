# Home Tutor Audit Playbook

## 10-minute audit flow
1. Search business name on Google.
2. Check website availability and mobile view.
3. Check Google Business Profile photos, reviews, services and contact.
4. Check Instagram/social trust.
5. Check WhatsApp click path.
6. List 3 quick improvements.

## Scorecard
- Online clarity: /10
- Trust proof: /10
- Booking flow: /10
- SEO/AEO readiness: /10
- Premium feel: /10

## Output format
Problem → Why it matters → Pragati Sahayak improvement → Next action.

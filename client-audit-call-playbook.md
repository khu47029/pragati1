# Client Audit Call Playbook

## Opening
Namaste, main Pragati Sahayak se bol raha hoon. Hum local businesses ka online presence audit karte hain.

## Ask
Aapka website/Google profile currently updated hai?

## Give value
Maine 2-3 quick improvement points note kiye hain jo online trust aur inquiry journey better kar sakte hain.

## CTA
Kya main WhatsApp par short audit points share kar doon?

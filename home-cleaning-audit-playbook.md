# Home Cleaning Audit Playbook

## Check these items
- Is the business category clear?
- Are services easy to understand?
- Are photos, reviews and location visible?
- Is there a WhatsApp or call CTA?
- Is the website mobile-friendly?
- Is there an FAQ section?
- Is the Google profile complete?

## Suggested pitch
Your business can look more premium online with a clear website, service presentation, reviews section and easy WhatsApp inquiry path.

PRACTICAL GUIDE

Google Business Profile Local Clients Kaise Dilata Hai?

Google profile local discovery ka strong trust point hai, especially near-me searches ke liye.

Direct answer

Google Business Profile business ko Google Search aur Maps par visible banata hai. Jab profile me services, photos, reviews, timings and contact clear hote hain, customer inquiry ka chance badhta hai.

Improve kaise kare?

  • Correct name, address and phone
  • Service categories
  • High-quality photos
  • Regular posts
  • Review collection
  • Website and WhatsApp links

Website ka role

Google profile customer ko website par bhej sakta hai. Website par service details, proof and booking CTA clear hona chahiye.

PRACTICAL GUIDE

Local Business Website Kyu Zaruri Hai in 2026?

Customer pehle online check karta hai. Website aapke business ka first trust point ban chuki hai.

Direct answer

Local business ko website isliye chahiye kyunki customer call, visit ya booking se pehle online trust check karta hai. Website services, pricing idea, reviews, photos, location and WhatsApp contact ko clear banati hai.

Website me kya hona chahiye?

  • Clear headline
  • Services and benefits
  • Reviews and proof
  • Gallery or portfolio
  • WhatsApp inquiry button
  • FAQ section
  • Google profile link

Best structure

Problem → Solution → Services → Proof → FAQ → WhatsApp CTA. Ye structure customer aur AI search systems dono ke liye clear hota hai.

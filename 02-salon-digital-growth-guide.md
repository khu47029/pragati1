# Salon Digital Growth Guide

## Direct answer
A salon should have a premium mobile website, Google profile, service presentation, review section, gallery, FAQ and WhatsApp booking CTA.

## Must-have website sections
- Hero: what you do and where you serve
- Services: clear names and benefits
- Trust: reviews, experience, photos
- Location: map and contact
- FAQ: direct answers
- CTA: WhatsApp inquiry

## AI Search Ready angle
Write pages in simple question-answer format and keep brand/service information consistent across website, social profiles and Google profile.

# Micro Growth Playbook 073

## Use case
A small, practical improvement idea for Pragati Sahayak website or client delivery.

## Action
Add one clear trust improvement: service clarity, review proof, photo proof, FAQ answer, WhatsApp CTA, Google profile update or case study.

## Why it helps
Small improvements make the website easier to understand for users and easier to parse for search and AI answer systems.

## Done when
The page has a clear heading, one useful answer, one trust signal and one CTA.

# City Page Guide

## Purpose
This document helps Pragati Sahayak keep the website professional, useful, premium and client-ready.

## Checklist
- Keep brand name consistent.
- Keep contact details unchanged unless intentionally updated.
- Add clear headings and direct answers.
- Use FAQ sections where useful.
- Add trust proof, process and CTA.
- Use schema where suitable.
- Keep pages mobile-friendly.
- Connect pages through internal links.
- Review before publishing.

## Output standard
The page should look premium, explain the service clearly and help a local business owner take the next step.

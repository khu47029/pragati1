Local SEO Checklist for Indian Local Businesses

Local SEO me sabse important hai Google profile, reviews, location clarity, service pages, photos, website speed aur WhatsApp inquiry.

  1. Google Business Profile complete karo
  2. Business name, address, phone consistent rakho
  3. Services clearly add karo
  4. Photos and gallery update karo
  5. Reviews collect karo
  6. Website par location + service pages banao
  7. FAQ and schema add karo
  8. WhatsApp CTA visible rakho

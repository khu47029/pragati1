WhatsApp Lead System Kya Hai?

Website visitor ko simple WhatsApp inquiry flow me convert karna hi WhatsApp lead system ka goal hai.

Direct answer

WhatsApp lead system website par aise buttons, forms and pre-filled messages set karta hai jisse customer ek tap me inquiry bhej sake.

System me kya hota hai?

  • Floating WhatsApp button
  • Service-specific WhatsApp CTA
  • Pre-filled message
  • Lead form
  • Follow-up script
  • Quick response templates

Local business ke liye benefit

Customer ko app change karke number save nahi karna padta. One tap me inquiry aa jati hai, jisse response fast hota hai.

# Doctor Clinic Online Trust Audit - Mathura

## Situation
A local doctor clinic needs a premium online presentation so customers can quickly understand services, reviews, location and contact options.

## Problems to check
- Website or landing page clarity
- Google Business Profile completeness
- Reviews and gallery proof
- WhatsApp inquiry flow
- Mobile speed and trust sections

## Pragati Sahayak improvement plan
1. Create a direct-answer homepage.
2. Add service cards and pricing/booking guidance where useful.
3. Add trust proof: reviews, gallery, owner intro, location.
4. Add FAQ and schema.
5. Connect WhatsApp inquiry.

## Expected business result
More trust, clearer customer journey and easier inquiry flow.
